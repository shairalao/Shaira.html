Mango Milktea

A fruity mango milk tea recipe that is the perfect bubble tea drink for summer. It’s like a creamier version of mango juice! Fruity, sweet & super refreshing, but with a soothing, creamier twist.It can be adjusted to taste, so the sweetness levels can range from slightly to very sweet.

INGREDIENTS

2 cups fresh or frozen mango. 1 cup cooled Jasmine Tea. 1 cup milk or coconut milk. 1 cup tapioca pearls. ¼ cup honey.
