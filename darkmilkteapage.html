Dark Milktea

Chocolate Milk Tea is high in cocoa which results in an irresistible smooth and rich texture! It's the ultimate comfort drink and you can make it hot or cold depending on your mood.

INGREDIENTS 3 Tablespoon of Chocolate Flavoured Powder. 1.5 Tablespoon Creamer or 60ml of your choice of milk. 3 Teaspoon sugar or any preferred sweetener. 1/2 Cup of Ice. 250ml hot water.
