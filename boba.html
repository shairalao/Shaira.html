Boba Milktea

This classic boba milk tea was inspired by my experiences walking around Taiwan. Every cup of boba milk tea had this signature condensed milk flavor that fused perfectly with the concentrated black tea.

INGREDIENTS

BOBA PEARLS ⅓ cup water(75 g), plus more as needed ¼ cup dark muscovado sugar(55 g), or brown sugar ¾ cup tapioca flour(65 g), plus more for dusting BROWN SUGAR SYRUP.

1 cup dark muscovado sugar(220 g), or brown sugar 1 cup water(240 mL) BLACK TEA.

2 cups water(480 mL) 6 black tea bags MILK MIXTURE.

3 tablespoons half & half 3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk ASSEMBLY.

3 cups ice(450 g), divided .
